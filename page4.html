WALES

Wales is a small, Celtic nation that is part of the United Kingdom, with a rich history of food and culture.

Welsh rarebit is the best cheese on toast you'll ever eat. Bread is grilled until cripsy, and a rich melted cheese is often enhanced by the addition of ale, mustard, or Worcestershire sauce.

Welsh cakes are a delicious snack or dessert. This short, sweet bread is shaped into small cylinders and mixed with raisins, and cooked on a hot plate until golden. A fresh sugar garnish is a must.

Bara brith is a loaf of spiced tea bread, containing dried fruits and spices. Meaning 'speckled bread' in Cymraeg, it is normally served with butter and a nice cup of tea.
