MEXICO

Mexico's distinctive cuisine is a favourite worldwide, with its bold spices and fresh flavours.

Chilli con carne is a spicy stew, made with ground meat (usually beef), kidney or pinto beans, and of course, chillis! Vegetables and spices such as garlic, onion, and cumin are often added, and it is commonly served with rice.

Burritos are versatile dish, consisting of a sealed wrap or 'tortilla', filled to the brim with vegetables, rice, meat, and sauces. Often served lightly toasted, they are a great meal for on the go, and you can eat with your hands.

A zesty and fresh dip made primarily of avocado, guacamole is a worldwide favourite. Lots of salt, lime juice, and coriander is added, as well as sometimes onions and chillis, to create a bright green, delicious condiment.
