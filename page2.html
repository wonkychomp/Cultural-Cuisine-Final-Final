ITALY

Italy is world renowned for its diverse and delicious cuisine. The birthplace of pizza, pasta, gelato... the list goes on!

Spaghetti bolognese is a pasta dish comprised of long, thin pasta and a rich, meaty sauce. Usually made with beef and pork, many vegetarian recipes have developed over recent years, using meat substitute or mushrooms as a base. Serve with fresh basil and parmesan cheese.

Pizza is a world famous dish that is comprised of dough, sauce (usually tomato!), cheese, and any other topping you can think of. The classic Margherita sticks just to cheese and sometimes basil, whereas the more adventurous Diavola mixes salami and chillis for some real heat.

Gelato is a dessert that consists of flavoured frozen cream and sugar. The authentic Italian option contains more air and milk, but less cream than other similar frozen desserts. It is also served warmer than typical ice creams. Popular flavours include hazelnut, coffee, and almond.
